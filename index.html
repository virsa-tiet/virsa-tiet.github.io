About us

Our culture, our tradition and our language are the foundations upon which we build our identity.We ought to fly in today’s competitive world, but we can only fly if our heart and soul are well connected to our roots.The advancement and development of our nations have given us ample facilities, but due to it, somewhere our roots, origin, and our heritages have been separated from us. We see more and more modern cultures invading our very own culture, due to which we ourselves too, have begin to forget our Virsa. Hence, one most needed society - “VIRSA-The Essence of Punjab” - is our contribution to the new generations, as well as, to the other civilizations, to showcase the ancient roots and origin of our richest culture - the Punjabi Virsa. Virsa takes full care that while you live your college years as your best years in Punjab, you fully grasp and savour The Essence of Punjab.
